Gambling simulation

This gambling simulator determines the ideal percentage of your total wealth to invest for a double or nothing betting situation at a given likelyhood of winning. The simulation starts with $100k for each run. There are 100 steps per run. 400 runs are performed at each investment percentage. Then the median of the last step of each run at a percentile is determined. Finally, the medians of all investment percentiles are compared. The percentile with the highest median is the recommended investment percentage.
